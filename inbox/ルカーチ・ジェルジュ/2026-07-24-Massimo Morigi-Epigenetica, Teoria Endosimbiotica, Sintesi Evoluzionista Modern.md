moment of an unexpected but powerful, interlaced, planetwide eruption of numerous
communities of a few hundred people each, who felt moved to migrate to ruined places and
work with human and nonhuman partners to heal these places, building networks, pathways,
nodes, and webs of and for a newly habitable world. Only a portion of the earthwide,
astonishing, and infectious action for well-being came from intentional, migratory
