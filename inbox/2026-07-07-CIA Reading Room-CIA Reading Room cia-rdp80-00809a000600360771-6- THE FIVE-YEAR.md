fSource: Neue Zuercher Zeitung, 25 Jul 50/

The Five-Year Plan of the German Democratic Republic represents the final
step in incorporating East Germany into the Soviet economic bloc. Fast Cermany

will inaugurate its Five-Year Plan on 1 January 1951, thereby falling in line with
the other Communist Satellites in economic planning.

The Five-Year Plan places its greatest emphasis on increased production in the
field of heavy industry. Five dismantled metallurgical plants with a combined ca-
