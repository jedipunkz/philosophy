8. Industry:

ae The power station of Oradea is located at 32 Strada Cetatii and con-
sists of the following three units:

1) A thermal unit with a four megawatt output;

2) Four disel generators (1,800, 2,00, and 3,600 horse power, re=
spectively) with a total output of six megawatts;

3) A hydroelectric power system which receives electric current from
the turbines at Astileu by way of the transformer station which is
also located at 32 Strada Cetatii. There are four turbines at
Astileu, all of Rumanian manufacture and of 1,200 hepe, each with
a total output of four megawatts. Their water power is supplied
by the dam at Vadul Crisului, 36 to 0 kilometers from Oradea, that
is connected to the turbines by a conduit about 12 kilometers longo

Oradea uses AC at 380 v. for industry and at 220 v. for domestic power
and lighting. The supply is free of breakdowns and in such quantity
that the station is acle to provide power to a number of villages
around Sarmasag as well.
