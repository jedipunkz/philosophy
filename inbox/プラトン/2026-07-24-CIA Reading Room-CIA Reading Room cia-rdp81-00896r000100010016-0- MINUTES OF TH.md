5. The Unit Chiefs presented their reactions to the
Project OUTREACH displays in ITG. All were agreed that the
"Energy"! exhibit needs a narration. In response to a question
from Chief, II, the DTR outlined the various exhibits which
were being prepared and, in general, their sequence. Most
members agreed that the displays were visually attractive,
although several members commented that the artwork was not
very professional in quality. Several members also pointed
out that the displays should be placed against the inner
wall of the first floor corridor, in order to avoid blocking
the visitors' view of the courtyard and to eliminate the
potential problem of glare from the windows interfering with
the displays.
