The Federacion Estudiantil Universitaria (FEU, Federation of University
Students) has issued a manifesto to the Cuban public stating that the FEU
will continue to oppose the BATISTA “dictatorship,” but that it will maintain
its independence from any political organization.

This stand is not endorsed by the more radical members of the student body
of Habana University, such as the Juventud Socialista (JS, Cuban Communist
Youth Organization), the Movimiento Nacional Revolucionario (MNR), the
Asociacion de Ambgos de Aureliano (AAA, Association of Friends of Aureliano),
the Ortodoxos, and the Liberacion Nacional,

Until the present, the neutral policy of the FEU president, German MORE, has
kept the divisions within the FEU working as one. The AAA, however, has not
given up the hope of gaining control of the university students. The

AAA candidates and sympathizers accepted the presidency of MORE as a
compromise candidate, with the intent of forcing him to resign. If through
threats this is accomplished, the leadership will probably go to Jose A,
ECHEVERRIA, who is the AAA leader in the FEU.

BE consents.

1. This policy does please, however, a large number of students, who would like
the university to keep completely aloof from political problems.

2. The AAA followers boast that if ECHEVERRIA gets control of the student
movement, "this school year will not be finished."
