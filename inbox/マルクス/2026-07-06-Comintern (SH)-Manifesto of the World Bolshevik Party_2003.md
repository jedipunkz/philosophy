Manifesto

of the
Party of World Bolshevism

[ - on occasion of the

100th anniversary of Bolshevism |

1903 - 2003

Wolfgang Eggers

Introduction

"A specter (presumed dead) is still haunting the world — the spectre of
Bolshevism.

All the imperialist powers and super powers, the powers of the world's reaction
and fascism, and all their lackeys did not desist from their hundred years old,
holy alliance to exorcise this specter."

Perhaps, those could be the words of Karl Marx and Friedrich Engels if they would write a new
"Manifesto of World-Bolshevism", today.
Well, we know that the founder of Bolshevism was their great successor, Comrade Lenin.

Over 100 years have passed in which the world bourgeoisie and the world reaction felt impelled to
repeat their tirades against Bolshevism for the thousandth time.

This shows that they have not stopped until today, to be afraid of Bolshevism and that they still
regard Bolshevism as their most dangerous enemy.

Nothing, even not the revisionists, can save world imperialism from the triumph of World-
Bolshevism .

It is time that we, the present-day Bolsheviks, dispel the old tale of the specter of Bolshevism -
namely forever.

Following in the words of the Communist Manifesto, we - the Bolsheviks - disdain to conceal our
views and aims.

We openly declare, there is only one reason why we Bolsheviks are still active:

Revolutionary overthrow of the entire imperialist world order!

Let the ruling world bourgeoisie tremble at our world Bolshevist revolution. The
world proletarians have nothing to lose but their chains. The world proletariat
has a world to win - the world of communism.

PROLETARIANS OF ALL COUNTRIES, UNITE!
WORLD PROLETARIAT - UNITE ALL COUNTRIES !

This is the purpose of this brief Manifesto of the World's Bolshevik Party, the Comintern (SH).

All over the world there will be no power that can stop us World Bolshevists, for we know how to
capture the global corridors of power and how to consolidate and maintain the victory of the world
socialist revolution.

What is

World-Bolshevism ?

a)

World-Bolshevism is ...

... the global, Marxist-Leninist theory and tactics of the world proletariat, of its
Bolshevik party, the Communist International (Stalinist-Hoxhaists) and its
Sections in all countries,

for complete and final, revolutionary destruction of the entire world capitalist
system,

the abolition of the world order of imperialism,

the realization of world socialism through the establishment of the dictatorship
of the world proletariat,

and finally to achieve the ultimate goal - the classless society of all mankind

- World Communism.

b)
World-Bolshevism is ...

... the tactical principle of the inevitability of the victory of proletarian class
struggle on an international scale according to the teachings of Marx, Engels,
Lenin, Stalin and Enver Hoxha,

and all this in spite of the fact that the world bourgeoisie has temporarily beaten
Bolshevism in the Soviet Union and in Albania - with the help of the revisionists.
World-Bolshevism is the grave-digger of World-Revisionism !

2

What is the meaning of

World-Bolshevism ?

The victory of World-Bolshevism -

That means the victory of the dictatorship of the proletariat on a world scale.

The victory of World-Bolshevism -

That means victory of the world proletariat over the bourgeoisie through the
socialist world revolution - the boiling point of global class struggle - guided by
the Comintern (SH) and its sections in every country .

The victory of World-Bolshevism -

- That means the expropriation of all banks in the world, of the multinational
corporations, of the world monopolies and of all capitalist private ownership at
the means of world production ( including at the natural resources and cultural

treasures of the world),

the expropriation of international large-scale landed property, manufacturing
and production equipment, etc.

- ultimately the abolition of the world order of capitalism through establishing a
new world order - the world-economic system of socialism.

The victory of World-Bolshevism -
That means the end of Wall Street and of the speculators in the world markets,
the final destruction of the capitalist exploitation and oppression,
the liberation of the people from the slavery of world imperialism,

the abolition of colonialism and neo-colonialism, the end of the division of the
world into dominant and dominated countries and nations,

the end of the epoch of superpowers and their global competing domination
blocks, etc., etc.

- that is freedom for all people - for every individual - the first time in human
history.

The victory of World-Bolshevism -

That means abolishing the inevitability of imperialist war and the abolition of
the inevitability of the restoration of fascism throughout the world - through the
direct transition of the combined economic struggle, the anti-fascist and anti-
war struggle against world imperialism into the socialist struggle for the world
proletarian revolution and the establishment of the world dictatorship of the
proletariat.

The victory of World-Bolshevism -

That means the material basis for a secure future of the working people
throughout the world who create their own new world without exploitation and
oppression.

The victory of World-Bolshevism -

That means world domination of the proletariat, the world government of
workers-peasants- and soldiers’ councils in all countries, the government of the
Soviet World Republic.

The victory of World-Bolshevism -

That means the correct globalized application of the teachings of the 5 Classics
of Marxism-Leninism,

That means continuation of the world-revolutionary path of the Comintern of
Lenin and Stalin - and its protection from renewed deviation into opportunism,
That means the victory of the Comintern (SH), the genuine successor
organization of the Comintern of Lenin and Stalin.

The victory of World-Bolshevism -

- That is the realization of the program of the Communist International from
1928 and the Manifesto of the Party of the world Bolshevism as it exists here

(and since 2009, additionally, the "Platform of the Comintern (SH)"

[world-programmatic declaration]

The victory of World-Bolshevism -

That means compliance and implementation of the general-line of the
Comintern (SH), and thus our new strategy and tactics of the proletarian,
socialist world revolution.

The triumph of Bolshevism - on a global scale -
is not yet proven.

But already proven is the victory of Bolshevism outside of its native country, the
Soviet Union.

Without restoration of Bolshevism in power (in Russia AND Albania), without

continuation of the great heritage of the Soviet Union and Albania, the victory of
Bolshevism on a world scale is impossible.

3
The program of

World-Bolshevism

(in brief)

The history of all hitherto existing society is the history of class struggles. The epoch of imperialism
is the epoch of moribund capitalism - first in one country and then around the world, is an epoch of
one of the highest developmental stages of the class struggle in the history of the class society.

Both the imperialist wars and crises as well as the realization of socialism in the Soviet Union and
Albania accelerated and shortened the course of world history, to replace the capitalist world order
by the socialist world order.

Today we have reached a point where the question of world revolution has become an issue of
practical solution when the entire world is facing the final battle:

World socialism or world barbarism.

In the history of the class struggle between the world bourgeoisie and the world proletariat
succeeded the proletarians initially by the victory of the October Revolution in Russia, through the
creation of the socialist camp of Stalin, and the victory of the anti-fascist revolution in Albania, to
establish the dictatorship of the proletariat in a single country. Thus, the inevitable end of the world
bourgeoisie was heralded historically, however not globally sealed in the very first step.

The world proletarian revolution could therefore not be performed to a victorious end, because the
world bourgeoisie temporarily managed to defeat socialism in a single country, to roll back the
socialist world camp of Stalin, to retake the power there again, and to restore capitalism.

A single socialist country, a number of emerging socialist countries, could be still kept in check, but
the world bourgeoisie will never be able to crush the universal, global revolution of the whole world
proletariat .

The Manifesto of World-Bolshevism

is the programme for the winning and establishing the dictatorship of the world
proletariat.

The objective laws of social development can neither be amended and converted nor repealed and
abolished.

The removal of world imperialism through world socialism is inescapably effected by the universal
law of the development of today's class society.

And the Manifesto of world Bolshevism is to accomplish this objective world
revolutionary development with the most effective forces and resources as well
as the quickest, shortest and simplest way, on a world scale.

The laws of political economy, whether they are active in capitalism or in socialism, are objective
laws, which determine all world-economic processes - beyond the influence of our will.

With the help of scientific socialism, the world proletariat is able to foresee the course of events in
the economic life of all peoples and nations. The world proletariat will outstandingly demonstrate
its ability for handling, guiding and mastering the global economy for its own benefit and for the
benefit of the whole world community.

The world proletariat will have the leading role in the whole political and economic development of
the world-socialist society.

The world proletariat will take over the world one day - in spite of current and past defeats and
setbacks. And no one can and will ever change anything about this fact.

We World-Bolcheviks can not predict, at what exact time world socialism will begin, but we know
with certainty that the world proletariat will transform the world socialism into an epochal fact, just
as we have already demonstrated the construction of socialism in two countries, the Soviet Union
and Albania, namely as an incontrovertible historical fact.

The capitalists have already globalized the forces of world production and its distribution, so that it

is ripe for its global socialization as the basis of the construction of the socialist world ecomic
system.

The decisive step is the revolutionary global annihilation of all existing capitalist relations of
production - namely without exception.

The difference between the world proletariat and the other classes in the world that have existed
throughout history, and which have revolutionized the relations of production, is that the world
proletariat is the only global class whose class interests merge with the interests of the vast majority
of the world population.

The world proletarian revolution does not mean abolition of one or another form of exploitation in
one or another country, however the abolishing of any kind of exploitation in all countries of the
world - without exception.

The revolutions of all other classes always remained within the narrow confines of their own class
interests, which were always in conflict with the interests of the majority of society.

In future, world socialism will create a new class structure, whose character has nothing in common
with the old well-known structure of the exploiting and exploited classes in the capitalist world. An
entirely new world proletariat will emerge through the revolutionary overcoming of the old
capitalist world. The world proletariat who has destroyed the capitalist world will not be any more
the same of that who will create the socialist world.

Even the victory of the proletarian dictatorship had created an entirely new class structure in the
history of mankind, the class structure of the socialist society in "one" country.

All exploiting classes were liquidated in socialism of "one" country. Only the working class, the
peasant class and the social stratum of the intelligentsia still remained.

In the capitalist world-economic system, the capitalist class possesses all means of world production
and - instruments with which the world proletariat and other classes are globally exploited.

Whereas the capitalist class in the socialist Soviet Union and in the socialist Albania was liquidated.
The means of production were in the hands of the workers, so that any possibility of exploitation of
the working class was excluded.

Socialism was implemented just as Karl Marx had once taught:

In order to free itself, the proletariat must smash the capitalist class, must confiscate and socialize
the capitalist private ownership at all means of production. Those relations of production which
enslave the proletariat have to be destroyed by the proletariat.

By doing this, the proletariat of the Soviet Union and Albania, transformed itself into a completely
new class, into a socialist working class, which has created socialist ownership for paving the way
towards communism.

Also the world proletariat as a global class will change itself into a world-socialist class.

Even the peasant class will be transformed by world socialism into a global socialist class.

In capitalist society, the farmers are a class of small producers, they belong to the middle class and
to the lower middle class in its vast majority. They are not to be mixed up with agricultural workers,
or respectively, with the poor of the village.

The petty bourgeoisie as a whole has a tendency to sink into the proletariat, because the livelihood
of the petty bourgeoisie is gradually deprived by the industrialization of the multinational
agricultural monopolies. The poor peasants are unpunished exploited by large land owners,
agricultural capitalists, and increasingly by multinational monopolies, who are the dominant owners
of global industrialized agriculture - as a characteristic phenomenon of today's globalization. These
relations of globalized agricultural production have to be destroyed, socialized and replaced by new
global forms of collective ownership.

The Soviet Union and Albania developed a peasantry that was free from exploitation. Private
property was no longer the basis of the economy of the peasantry, however the collective property,
which developed on the basis of collective work.

In the advanced period of socialism the socialist state began to replace successively the collective
property by the public state property - striving for communism.

Only in the epoch of world socialism the petty bourgeoisie ceases globally to be a privileged class
in face of the world proletariat. It merges with the working class before it ceases to exist. However,
the world proletariat will not forget that the petty bourgeoisie will never abandon its role as a source
of the restoration of capitalism. Therefore the dictatorship of the world proletariat is still needed
even if the bourgeoisie was abolished long ago.

World socialism means globalization of the farmers' collective property.

World Socialism shields the property of all peoples from the restoration of capitalist property. This
will be guaranteed by the globalization of the public property of the peoples.

It thus begins the era of the complete fusion of all the state-owned property of the nations by the
commonly existing world-state-owned property.

The Manifesto of the World Bolshevist Party states:

In a socialized world the public property of the peoples will be transformed into
the public property of the world state, before both the state and property will be
completely abolished in world Communism.

The globalization of capitalism leads to the intensification of the contradiction between town and
country.

The globalization of socialism leads to the reduction and ultimate elimination of the contradiction
between town and country.

Socialism has given rise to a new social stratum of the working intelligentsia that never did exist in

previous times. Under capitalism, and even in the pre-capitalist period the intelligentsia was the
servant of the rich. This privileged social stratum was recruited mainly from the classes of the rich.

In socialist society, there was no more exploiting class and consequently no demand for its servants.
Under socialism, the intelligentsia was servant of the working class. And the new toiling
intelligentsia was mainly recruited from the working class and the farmers.

In the new socialist class structure, the boundaries between the working class and the peasantry
blurred gradually. And this concerns also the boundaries between the working class and the
collective farmers, on the one hand, and the new social stratum of the intelligentsia, on the other
hand. The old exclusivity of the capitalist class society vanished.

A characteristic feature of the socialist class structure was the fact that, first, the distance between
these social groups decreased more and more, and secondly, that both the economic contradictions
and political differences between these social groups blurred more and more. Under the leadership
of the ruling working class they formed united forces for achieving the common goal of
communism.

The socialist class structure included both remnants of the old class structure and already features of
the future classless society. We can speak with complete justification of a class structure that
represents the transition from the old to the new classless society - initially on a national scale and
in the second period of socialism on an international scale.

That this development could only take place in the sharp class struggle against the remnants of the
old classes should be highlighted explicitly. Finally, the historical fact is well-known that the
revisionist class structure restored the bourgeois class structure.

By the temporary defeat of socialism, the socialist class structure was destroyed, indeed. But it
leaves still deep, indelible traces. The post-socialist capitalism cannot cope with the glorious
heritage of the golden years of the past. No one can judge the advantages of socialism over
capitalism clearer than the workers of the former socialist countries. Nobody in the world will
therefore struggle more consistently for the restoration of socialism.

The people who once have built socialism in their country, will be the first to fight for the world
socialist revolution. Only world socialism is able to overcome the avoidable and unavoidable
mistakes of the first period of socialism.

The restoration of socialism, realized by the creation of world socialism , this is
one of the pillars of the Manifesto of the World Bolshevik Party.

Today, the antagonistic class contradictions have globalized. It is the epoch of the sharpest and
grimmest class struggles in which the whole world is completely involved.

There is an extreme polarization of two globalized classes. The global class struggle between the
world bourgeoisie and the world proletariat will decide the future of humankind. These two classes
determine the fate of the world. While the world proletariat represents the vast majority of the world
population, the world bourgeoisie represents only a small minority.

Either the world bourgeoisie will win - and then world capitalism will overthrow the earth into the
abyss. Or the world proletariat will win and build a new world without exploitation and oppression.
There are no other alternatives.

Therefore, this Manifesto calls for the united front of all progressive world
forces to participate in the final global battle of the world proletariat against the
world bourgeoisie.

The world bourgeoisie stirs up the conflicts of nations to maintain their global power.
The world proletariat unites all countries for the overthrow the capitalist world power.

Only through unification in the world socialist revolution will the workers of all countries be
victorious !

Workers of the world - unite!

World proletariat - unite all countries !

The globalization of world capitalism is presupposition for the globalization of world socialism.

Without capitalist globalization - no socialist globalization !

Globalization of capitalism means more enrichment of the rich nations, on the one hand, and much
more impoverishment of the poor nations, on the other hand.

Globalization of Socialism means minimizing and elimination of the contradiction between the rich
and poor nations.

World socialism will be constructed by the united forces of the proletarians and their allies of all
nations, and everything what they jointly produce, will be distributed among themselves equitably:

»Each country according to its benefits -

each country according to its needs !“

The spontaneous consciousness of the world proletariat develops towards the world-communist
class-consciousness by means of the world Bolshevist party which is the bearer of modern socialist
science. The teachings of the 5 Classics of Marxism-Leninism are basis and guide line for the
conscious class struggle of the workers' world movement.

By the organized combination of the doctrine of Marxism-Leninism with the real world labor
movement, with the application of the ideas of proletarian internationalism in the international
centralized class struggles, the world proletariat creates the necessary subjective conditions for the
triumph of the world proletarian revolution by means of the leadership of its World Bolshevik Party.

The Manifesto of the World Bolshevist Party states:

The proletarian world revolution is the only way out of the capitalist world
crises

World capitalism and world crises belong together like Jack belongs to Jill. However, the current
world crisis is the deepest and most critical one in the whole history of capitalism. It is a crisis of
the globalized world that has shaken the entire system of capitalism up to its foundation, but
ultimately to bring world capitalists to their knees - this is only possible by the socialist revolution
of the world proletariat - by no one else!

The current world crisis does not only weaken the economical position of the world bourgeoisie.
Consequently also its political position begins to totter.

In this situation it is the task of the world proletariat to take advantage of the current weakness of
world capitalism and to seize the opportunity, to unite its forces in joint revolutionary actions.

Lenin seized his opportunity successfully in the situation of the weakness of world imperialism
which was once caused by the first imperialist world war.

World Bolshevists of today must seize the opportunity to take advantage of the current world crisis
which causes the weakness of the world imperialists.

This means to follow the Bolshevist path of comrade Lenin !

The World Bolshevist program is not only a program for the overthrow and
expropriation of the capitalists all over the world, but also a program to build
the world-socialist economy.

From the proletarian gravediggers of capitalism in one country emerge the gravediggers of the
entire international world capitalism.

The world proletariat will not and cannot stand idly until the world imperialists will have ruined
entirely all livelihood.

The working class will forestall D-day to keep afloat and not going down with the world
bourgeoisie. It is vital for the existence of the world proletariat to stand up for the overthrow of the
world bourgeoisie. The world proletariat will therefore not miss a single chance for the proletarian
seizure of power for the purpose of quickly replace the destructive and ruined capitalism by a new
world socialist economy.

One day the global products will be common property of all peoples of the world. They will be used
to fulfill the most urgent needs and demands of all the peoples.

Only by strict limitation of the spheres of the mode of the functioning of the international law of
value and its restricted role as a regulator of global production can the inevitability of the world
recurring capitalist crises of overproduction be overcome.

The law of value is effective as a regulator of the production through the conditions of the
circulation of commodities, through existence of private ownership at the global means of
production , through competion at the world markets, through the anarchy of world production,
through crises of overproduction, and especially through buying and selling labor force at the labor
markets, etc.

In the world-socialist economy the sphere of action of the law of value will be drastically reduced
by the presence of the global social ownership of the means of production, by the power of the law
of planned and balanced development of the world economy.

The value is - equal to the law of value - a historical category that is related to the existence of
commodity production. If commodity production disappears, then also both the value (in all its
forms) and the law of value will disappear.

The Manifesto of the World Bolsgevist Party states:

We strive for the abolition of the world society which is based on selling and
buying commodities. Primarily we strive for the abolition of the commodity of
labor.

What we strive for is the direct global exchange of globally produced goods.

Marxism-Leninism argues that the transition from socialism to communism and the communist
principle of distribution of the products expressively excludes any exchange of commodities.
Consequently, communism excludes expressively any exchange of products into commodities and
with it their transformation into value.

In the second phase of communist society, the spent labor for the production of global goods will
not be measured crabwise, thus not by means of the value and its forms as it was the case in the
production of commodities.

Communist labor will be measured directly and straight by the amount of time, by the number of
hours that has been used for the fabrication of products .

Concerning the distribution of labor, the distribution of labor will not be regulated by the law of
value (which is no longer in force) between the international branches of production, but by the
growing product demand of the world community.

So this will be a world where the production is regulated by the needs of the world population, and
where the inclusion of the needs of the world's population will become of prime importance for the
world-socialist Five-Year-Plan, for international planning institutions, for a global planning
commission etc. in which the representatives of the peoples of the world have a say.

Then the world of capitalist production, the times of the exploitation of the world labor, the profit-
driven times, will disappear forever. The economic law of capitalism will no longer be operative.

What is the purpose of the capitalist world production?

Marx explains the pure purpose of capitalist production:

"The direct purpose of capitalist production is not production of goods, but the surplus value or
profitability in its developed form, not the product, but the surplus product. From this standpoint
labour itself is only productive if it creates profit or surplus product for the capital.

As far as the worker cannot make this, his/her labour is unproductive. Thus, the volume of
applied productive labour is only to such degree interesting for the capital, as the volume of
surplus labour grows. It is the permanent purpose of capitalist production to fabricate a
maximum of surplus value or surplus production by means of a minimum of advanced capital.
And as far as this result is not achieved through excessive labour of the workers, there is a
tendency of capital, by which it strives for creating a given product by means of most possible
lowest efforts - namely economizing on labour and costs ...

According to this view the workers appear themselves as that what they really are in the capitalist
mode of production - a mere means of production, not as an end in itself and not as purpose of
production (see: "Theories of Surplus Value, Volume IJ, Part 2).

As for the consumption, so capitalism needs it only in so far as it satisfies the purpose of creating
profit. Apart from this fact, the question of consumption of the world's population loses every sense
for capitalism. The people and their needs disappeared from the world of capitalism.

What is the purpose of the socialist world production?

Care for the world population and its needs is the most important purpose of the world of socialism.

Purpose of the world-socialist production is not profit, but to satisfy the needs of all people in the
world, ie, the satisfaction of both their material and cultural needs.

Engels formulated:

After Capitalism "a socially-planned regulation of production according to the needs of all and
of each individual - replaces the social anarchy of production. With this the capitalist mode of
appropriation (in which the product firstly enslaves the producer, but then also the appropriator)
is replaced, by the mode of appropriation of the products which, in itself, is based in the nature of
the modern means of production:

The one hand, direct social appropriation as a means of preserving and expanding the
production, on the other hand direct individual appropriation as food and pleasure average"
(Friedrich Engels' Anti-Dihring").

It's no longer about the ruinous securing a capitalist maximum profit through exploitation, ruin and

impoverishment of the majority of the world population, by subjugation and systematic plundering

of the peoples of all countries, especially the colonial and neo-colonial countries, and by imperialist
wars and militarization of the world economy.

Instead of securing maximum profits = securing of the maximum satisfaction of
the material and cultural needs of a global society;

instead of critical decline in production, interruptions by periods of crises =
continuous expansion of production related to the growth and needs of the world
population;

instead of destruction of the world-productive forces (accompanied by
imperialist wars, crises of overproduction and periodic interruptions in the
development of the technology) = continuous improvement of global production
on the basis of the world's high technology and highest standard of
environmental friendliness.

The law of planned development of the world economy can only fully develop if it is based on and
applied to the economic law of world socialism, which will be effective both in every single country
and on a global scale:

Stalin formulated:

"The essential features and requirements of the basic law of socialism might be formulated
roughly in this way: the securing of the maximum satisfaction of the constantly rising material
and cultural requirements of the whole of society through the continuous expansion and
perfection of socialist production on the basis of higher techniques."

(Economic Problems of the USSR; 7. The Basic Economic Laws of Modern Capitalism and of
Socialism)

The long-term aim of the Manifesto of the World-Bolshevist Party:

- transition to world communism

As for the reproduction, so Karl Marx had postulated that his theory of reproduction is also valid for
the socialist production. Thus, it is also basis for the world-socialist reproduction. In his "Critique of
the Gotha Programme" Karl Marx analyzed the economics of socialism and the transition period to
communism and adopted the most important theses of his theory of reproduction, which he

regarded as mandatory in a communist society. This also applies to Engels in his "Anti-Dihring".

For the transition to world communism, at least three basic preconditions must
be fulfilled:

1.

First, it is necessary to secure steady growth of total world production by preferential growth
of production of means of production.Preferential growth of production of means of
production is not only necessary because they must supply both the companies in each
country, as well as the companies in all other countries with equipment, but also because
otherwise there is no expanded global reproduction.

2.

Second, it is necessary, to raise the collective property onto the level of general world
property (by gradual transitions which are advantageous for both the individual collective
farms in a country and for the collective farms in all countries, and consequently also for the
entire world population).

And the commodity circulation must be replaced by a system of exchange of products
among all countries ( also by gradual transitions).

A central world authority is needed, or any other socio-economic world headquarter which is
able to acquire and evaluate the total world production of products and its distribution, in the
interest of the world's population.

3:

Third, it is necessary to achieve such a high cultural growth of the world socialist society
which provides, to all the members of the world community the all-round development of
their overall physical and mental potential.

The members of the socialist world community should receive an education that is sufficient
for being active participants in the new social world development and to allow them to make
their free choice in all their activities.

Nobody shall be tied to a particular field of activity in all his/her life - by virtue of the
existing division of labor in different countries.

The individual socialist countries require enough space and free time to develop their
cultural growth.

These are the three main conditions for the preparation of the transition to world
communism. Only after the fulfillment of all these conditions in its entirety is to be hoped
that the work in the eyes of all members of world socialism changes from a heavy load
towards "the first desire of life" (Karl Marx) and that the heavy load of labor will become a
"pleasure" (Friedrich Engels ).

The social ownership shall be considered as unshakable and inviolable basis of existence of
