A Summary of All My Works

By Karl Marx (1871 - 1878)

Table of Contents

I. Marxism and how this is about
Marxism

II. Marx and Soviet works, in
Progress Publishers describing
the Marxist as a nature loving
person.

III. Marxism and the Practice of
the Party in Gotha

IV. Marxism in this Account as
Partial Still

V. Marxism as in fact Clear Cut
Dogmatic Behaviour

VI. Marxism then as Soviet
Leninism.

I. Paris Commune, Class
Struggles in France, England
and Germany and First
International as History and
Lived Experience - How to be
about the Process of the
Militant as a Historian

I mean be a militant and cover this
process of living history and be
interested in the more abstract
character of social affairs.

II. Anthropological Stance of the
Margins and Sub-Margins

Then articulate this to say Japanese
views on society and social
production process and be
interested in also tribal history. I
mean it is fundamental to be
natural.

III. Naturalism, Philosophy of
Nature, Ecology

I mean we are free natural people,
with free minds and free thoughts.
Anarchism as well.

IV. Anarchism and Proudhonists -
How they are to be admired but
also criticized

I mean anarchism is true, but also
Marxism is truer but they can be
seen together.

V. Grundrisse and Capital - How
the Militant then joins the
Jewish Question

I mean then German words, like
uproot and uproot entirely. I also
mean be an uprising and be a

militant who sits and reads the
newspaper daily.

VI.Mind as Concrete and Outside
the Mind as Materialism

Be concrete and in the mind a bit,
but be a free mind, as free as youth.
I mean though be a aspiration to
totality, cover the totality.

VII. Observations on People and
Experiences of Boulevards -
Qualities not Quantities finally -
Crosses all over the Boulevard

I will live forever, I think of quality is
true, truer than quantities, though
quantities Lincoln respects as
accounts.

Travel New York sometime, and see
black people with white people in
liberal air.

Friedrich Engels
