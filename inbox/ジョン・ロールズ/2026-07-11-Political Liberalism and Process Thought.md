## Abstract

<p>Chapter Abstract: This chapter continues the effort to bring together the twin concepts of 'political liberalism' and 'process thought' by tracing the origin and history of political liberalism and by detailing the politically liberal views of Whitehead and Hartshorne.  The emphasis is on the ongoing character of both political liberalism and the method by which politically liberal principles are justified.</p>
